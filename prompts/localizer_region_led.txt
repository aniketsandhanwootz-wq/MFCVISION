Look for the narrow black inset display window that contains the dim placeholder 8s and the bright green or orange active digits.
Box the full inner display window only. Do not box the whole blue fascia or the whole front face of the machine.
The correct target sits below the bowl or pan and above the round control buttons.

ANTI-TARGETS
- Do not include the bowl, pan, or metal platform above.
- Do not include the round control buttons below.
- Do not include the full lower machine body.
- Do not include the full blue fascia if the black inset window is smaller inside it.

The correct box should tightly isolate the dark glass window that contains the LED digits and placeholder segments.
